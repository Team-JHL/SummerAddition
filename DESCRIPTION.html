Summer Addition ☀️

Summer Addition adds a vibrant, summery atmosphere to Minecraft by introducing a variety of new items, blocks, and gameplay features inspired by the joys of the season.

Features 🌿🍅🏰

  • Sand Shovel: A special tool that lets you interact with sand in new ways — build and collect unique sand structures like sand castles using this shovel.
  • New Plants: Discover and harvest wild tomato bushes and parsley plants found naturally in plains biomes, adding more farming variety to your world.
  • Delicious Food: Cook tomato stew using fresh ingredients, and enjoy a selection of colorful ice lollies and ice cream cones with different flavors to cool you down on hot days.
  • Custom Creative Tab: Easily access all new items and blocks through a dedicated summer-themed creative mode tab.
  • Summer-themed Painting: Decorate your builds with a new painting capturing the essence of summer.

Achievements 🏆

Unlock fun and thematic advancements as you explore the mod’s content, like crafting the sand shovel, building all sand structures, finding wild tomatoes and parsley, and mastering the art of enjoying summer treats without brain freeze!

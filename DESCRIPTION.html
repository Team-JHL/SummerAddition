Summer Addition ☀️

Summer Addition brings a bright, relaxed summer vibe into Minecraft, expanding the game with new plants, foods, tools, and decorative content inspired by the warmth and creativity of the season.

Overview 🌿🍅🏰

Explore plains biomes to find new wild crops like tomato bushes and parsley, which expand your farming options and can be used to create delicious new food. From hearty tomato stew to refreshing ice lollies and ice cream cones, Summer Addition adds a flavorful collection of seasonal treats to enjoy during your adventures.

A special Sand Shovel also changes how you interact with sand, allowing you to shape and build unique structures like sand castles. To complement the world’s atmosphere, the mod includes a summer-themed painting and a dedicated creative tab that keeps all new content neatly organized.

Progression 🏆

As you play, you’ll unlock themed advancements that guide you through the mod’s content — from crafting your first sand tools to discovering wild crops, building sand structures, and trying out all summer-inspired foods.
